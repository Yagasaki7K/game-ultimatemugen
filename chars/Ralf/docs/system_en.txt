-- Basic Moves --
Light punch				X
Hard kick				Y
Light kick				A
Hard kick				B
Strike attack				C
Emergency evade				X+AorZ
Taunt					Start
Charge power				2+Start(can hold)

-- Dash --
You can configure "config.txt" to change dash-type.
Or push start duaring first round intro.

-DASH
Dash					66
Quick emergency evade			(during normal attack)X+AorZ

-STEP
Walk speed is more faster than other type.
Step					66
Quick emergency evade			(during normal attack)X+AorZ

-G.STEP
Grand step				66
Guard-Cancel grand step			(while blocking)66
Attack-Cancel grand step		(during normal attack)X+AorZ

-- Jump --
Small jump				(tap)7or8or9
Middle jump				(tap)1or2or3,(tap)7or8or9
Long jump				(tap)1or2or3,7or8or9

-- Maximum mode --
If you want to change button combination like original game(Y+A),
change the paramater in "config.txt".

Maximum mode(KOF'98)			2+Y+B
Maximum mode(KOF'02)			Y+B
Quick Maximum mode			(during normal attack)Y+B
  ->Quick dash				(hold)6

-- Guard Cancel --
Guard-Cancel strike attack		(while blocking)C
Guard-Cancel emergency evade		(while blocking)X+AorZ

-- Special Cansel --
Super-Cancel				Cancel super move into DM or super DM.
Dream-Cancel				Cancel DM into super DM.

-- Cost of power level --
Guard-Cancel grand step			1-gauge
Guard-Cancel strike attack		1-gauge
Guard-Cancel emergency evade		1-gauge
Cancel emergency evade			1-gauge
Attack-Cancel grand step		1-gauge
Maximum mode				1-gauge
Quick Maximum mode			1.5-gauge
DM					(normally)1-gauge / (during Maximum mode)end of Maximum mode
Extra DM				(normally)1.5-gauge / (during Maximum mode)0.5-gauge+end of Maximum mode
Super DM				(normally)2-gauge / (during Maximum mode)1-gauge+end of Maximum mode
Hidden DM				(normally)3-gauge / (during Maximum mode)2-gauge+end of Maximum mode
